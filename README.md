## 🚀 How to Use These Prompts

Each prompt file contains 4 prompts organized by use case. Here's how to get the most out of them:

**1. Fill in every `[bracketed]` field.**  
Vague input = vague output. The more specific you are, the better the AI response.

**2. Choose the right model.**  
These prompts are designed for Claude, ChatGPT GPT-4, or Gemini Advanced. Lighter models may produce weaker results for complex prompts.

**3. Iterate.**  
Use the AI's first output as a draft, not a final answer. Ask follow-up questions like:
- *"Make this shorter and more executive-friendly."*
- *"Rewrite the risk section to be more specific to our timeline."*
- *"Add a section on customer impact."*

**4. Save your best outputs.**  
When an AI response lands well, save it. Over time, your real outputs become better source material than the prompts themselves.
